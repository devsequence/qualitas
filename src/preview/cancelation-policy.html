Cancelation Policy

Please note that once you have booked a lesson with us it means that we have reserved time in our schedule exclusively for you. We request that you cancel at least 48 hours before a scheduled class. Any reservations missed, late cancelled, or changed without 48 hour notice will result in a charge equal to 100% of the reserved service amount.

You can cancel or reschedule an appointment by emailing us at brooklyndjstudio@gmail.com or calling / texting our office at +1(646) 591-9789.

Brooklyn DJ School reserves right to refuse service to anyone.
